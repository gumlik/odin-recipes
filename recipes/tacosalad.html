Taco Salad

The road to a bowl of tacos!

This taco salad recipe will show you how to create this wonderful, culinary treat. We will bring flavors from south of the border to the tip of your tongue. Get out your maracas and put on some cumbias because tonight we're here fiesta...taco salad style.
  • Corn Tortilla Chips
  • Ground Beef/Turkey
  • Salad Greens
  • Red Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cut up your lettuce, onions, tomato, cilantro and throw it all in a big bowl and top it with corn chips
  2. Cook your ground meat in a pan until it is well cooked
  3. Add 3 skadooshes of tomato sauce and 4 skadooshes of water. Add your taco seasoning and stir
  4. Pour over your prepared salad bowl, add your cheese and sour cream and stir it all together
